How It Works

Craft and offer your own exclusive NFT creations for sale.

  • Create your wallet

    After setting up your preferred wallet, link it to OpenSea by selecting the NFT Marketplace option in the top right corner. Familiarize yourself with the wallets we accommodate.

  • Craft unique collection

    Click on "Create" and establish your collection. Incorporate social links, descriptions, profile and banner images, and specify a secondary sales fee.

  • Upload your NFTs

    Share your work (image, video, audio, or 3D art), provide a title and description, and personalize your NFTs with attributes, statistics, and unlockable features.

  • Sell your NFTs

    Select from auctions, fixed-price listings, or declining-price listings to tailor your NFT sales strategy to your preferences.
